Research

I am interested in atypical neurobiological processes involved in the development of psychiatric disorders. My work focuses on integrative neuroimaging-heavy data analysis to map multilevel neurobiological correlates of typical and atypical phenotypes, from behavior, to brain-regional, molecular and genetic processes.
