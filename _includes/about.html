About Me

Portrait @ Nikolaus Brade

I am a PhD-doctoral candidate in developmental neuroscience and psychiatry and have a background in medicine. For my MD, I worked on resting-state functional MRI in Anorexia nervosa.

I am currently participating in a four-year interdisciplinary Clinician Scientist PhD-program at the Max Planck School of Cognition and am affiliated with Jürgen Dukart's Biomarker Development Group at Jülich Research Centre and University Hospital Düsseldorf, Germany.
